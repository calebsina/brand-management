About Us

Bridging the Digital Divide through Innovation since 2021

At Starlight Inc., we are passionate about empowering individuals and communities through technology and education. Founded with a vision to bridge the digital divide, we are dedicated to providing innovative solutions that foster learning, creativity, and growth in the tech industry.

Our team is comprised of experienced professionals and educators who are committed to making a difference. We believe that everyone deserves access to the tools and knowledge needed to succeed in today’s fast-paced digital world. Through our flagship projects—Smartly, Nextgen, and KITADIS—we strive to create opportunities for people of all ages to explore, learn, and excel in technology.

Whether it’s offering cutting-edge training for aspiring developers, nurturing the next generation of tech enthusiasts, or providing accessible educational resources, Starlight Inc. is at the forefront of driving positive change through technology.

Join us on our journey to illuminate the path to a brighter future for all.
